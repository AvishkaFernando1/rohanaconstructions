Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of construction projects do you handle?

Rohana Construction specializes in a wide range of projects including commercial buildings, residential complexes, industrial facilities, and renovation work. We have the expertise and resources to handle projects of various scales and complexities.

How do I request a quote for my construction project?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form on this page, calling our office directly, or sending us an email with details about your project. Our team will get back to you within 24-48 hours to discuss your requirements and provide a comprehensive quote.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope, complexity, and specific requirements of each project. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline that includes all phases from planning and design to construction and completion.

Do you offer sustainable and eco-friendly construction options?

Yes, we are committed to sustainable building practices. We offer various eco-friendly options including energy-efficient designs, sustainable materials, and green building certifications like LEED. Our team can guide you through the available options to make your project more environmentally responsible.
