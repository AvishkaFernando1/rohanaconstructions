Azure Tower

Commercial Office Complex

Downtown Business District
Completed: June 2022
Area: 45,000 sq ft

Project Overview

Azure Tower is a state-of-the-art commercial office complex designed to provide a premium workspace environment for modern businesses. The 15-story building features floor-to-ceiling windows, offering panoramic city views and abundant natural light.

Key Features

  • LEED Gold certified sustainable design
  • Smart building technology with automated climate control
  • Rooftop garden and recreational space
  • Underground parking with EV charging stations
  • High-speed elevators and advanced security systems

Construction Highlights

The construction utilized innovative techniques including prefabricated structural elements to reduce on-site construction time by 30%. The building's distinctive curved glass facade required specialized installation methods and custom-manufactured panels.

Skyline Plaza

Shopping Mall & Entertainment Center

Central Shopping District
Completed: March 2023
Area: 75,000 sq ft

Project Overview

Skyline Plaza is a premier shopping and entertainment destination featuring over 150 retail outlets, a multiplex cinema, and a diverse food court. The complex was designed to create an immersive shopping experience with spacious walkways and natural lighting.

Key Features

  • Multi-level retail space with premium brand outlets
  • 12-screen cinema complex with IMAX technology
  • Interactive digital directories and smart parking system
  • Central atrium with seasonal displays and events space
  • Energy-efficient lighting and climate control systems

Construction Highlights

The construction of Skyline Plaza incorporated a unique steel and glass dome structure that required specialized engineering solutions. The project utilized BIM (Building Information Modeling) throughout the construction process to coordinate complex systems integration and minimize conflicts.

Heritage Hotel Renovation

Historical Building Restoration

Old Town District, City Center
Completed: July 2022
Area: 35,000 sq ft

Project Overview

The Heritage Hotel Renovation project involved the careful restoration of a 120-year-old historic building while converting it into a luxury boutique hotel. The project balanced preservation of architectural heritage with modern hospitality requirements.

Key Features

  • Restoration of original facade and architectural details
  • 42 uniquely designed guest rooms and suites
  • Modern amenities integrated with historical elements
  • Seismic retrofitting for structural safety
  • Rooftop terrace with panoramic city views

Modern systems including HVAC, plumbing, and electrical were carefully integrated to minimize impact on historical features while meeting current building codes and comfort standards.

Urban Loft Conversion

Warehouse to Residential Transformation

Arts District, Downtown
Completed: February 2023
Area: 28,000 sq ft

Project Overview

The Urban Loft Conversion project transformed a century-old industrial warehouse into 18 luxury residential lofts. The design preserved the building's industrial character while creating contemporary living spaces with modern amenities.

Key Features

  • Open floor plans with 14-foot ceilings and exposed structural elements
  • Energy-efficient windows that maintain the original industrial aesthetic
  • Rooftop garden with community gathering spaces
  • Ground floor retail and art gallery spaces
  • Preserved original brick walls and timber beams

Construction Highlights

The conversion required extensive structural reinforcement while preserving the building's historic character. New mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems were integrated within the existing structure, requiring innovative solutions to maintain the loft aesthetic while meeting modern building codes.
